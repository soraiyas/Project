POPART MCGILL

The desire to create is one of the deepest yearnings of the human soul. - Dieter F. Uchtdorf

We believe that everyone has an inner child-like artist that is a worthy adversary for even our loudest inner critic. Through spontaneous, creative, all-inclusive art projects on campus, PopArt McGill encourages the McGill community to disconnect from expectations and invest in strengthening our true inner child.

We noticed that McGill offers very few outlets to express students' creative sides.
So a group of students came together to brainstorm ways to incorporate art into McGill's public spaces.
Studies show that art is essential to living a healthier life.
NO BRAINER
Reclaim your inner artist. Silence your inner critic.

Stop by the Brown Building starting this Thursday through the end of the semester to give your beautiful brain a break, have fun coloring Mandalas, and maybe some random dance parties?
